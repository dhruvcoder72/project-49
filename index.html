Facts about camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.

Facts about chameleon.

  • A Chameleon's eyes can swivel 180 degrees
  • Chameleons like warm, tropical environments like the rain forest
  • Chameleons like to hang around in trees

Facts about pigeon

  • Dodos(extinct bird) were related to todays pigeons
